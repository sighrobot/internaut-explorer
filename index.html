  •   About

      Internaut Explorer is an experimental visualization of DMOZ, an open-content directory. The interactive map includes 3,809,444 pages and 782,239 topics from February 2015, used under CC BY 3.0.

      Special thanks to CARTO for their generous support of this project.

  •   Help

      Greetings, Internaut! You just materialized in a random sector of the Web.

      Click, drag and zoom (just as you would on a map for Earthlings) to explore the veritable unknown.

      If you find yourself lost in space, refresh your browser to be instantly transported to another random sector.
